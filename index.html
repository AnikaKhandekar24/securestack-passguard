03 / SAFER HABITS

Small changes. Stronger security.

Good password habits protect more than one account.

01

Make it long

Aim for 14+ characters. A memorable phrase is easier to recall and harder to guess.

02

Use unique passwords

Never recycle passwords. One breach should not unlock your other accounts.

03

Turn on MFA

Add a second verification step. It can stop an attacker even if your password leaks.
